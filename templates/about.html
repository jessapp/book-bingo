What is Book Bingo?


It's bingo, but with a reading twist - each square corresponds to a genre, and when you read a book that fits that genre, fill the square in with the book's title and author. When you've read books corresponding to five squares in a row, you get bingo! Use it to set reading goals for yourself, or play against friends.

How do I play?

Sign up for an account, and create a new board. Choose any 24 genres that you want! (Can't think of 24 genres? Don't worry - there are plenty of suggestions.) Once you have a board, get ready to start reading!
