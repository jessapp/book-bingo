What is Book Bingo?


It's bingo, but with a reading twist - each square corresponds to a genre, and when you read a book that fits that genre, fill the square in with the book's title and author. When you've read books corresponding to five squares in a row, you get bingo! Use it to set reading goals for yourself, or play against friends.

How do I play?


Sign up for an account and create a new board to get started. Choose any 24 genres that you want! (Can't think of 24 genres? Don't worry - there are plenty of suggestions.) Once you have a board, get ready to start reading! To enter a book in a genre square, click "Explore This Genre" and enter your book's information.

How do I share a board?


It's easy to play with friends! Copy the share link at the bottom of the board you want to share, and send it to a friend. When they log in through that link, they'll be added to the board.

That description isn't for the book I read! What's going on?

Book Bingo uses the Goodreads API to find descriptions. If your book has a common title, it's possible that it didn't find the right book. This issue will be addressed in version 2.0!
